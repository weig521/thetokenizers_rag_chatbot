## Holds

Listed below are the common holds that could prevent registration or the release of
transcripts and diplomas, along with general instructions for addressing the hold.
If you have a hold that is not on this list, you can find more information on the
Registration Status page of your [Student Self-Service](https://bannersso.usf.edu/ssomanager/c/SSB) account.

**\*Note:** Students with a hold that prevents adding, dropping or withdrawing from class sections
may contact asktheregistrar@usf.edu from their USF email to drop or withdraw if the [applicable deadline](https://www.usf.edu/registrar/calendars/index.aspx) has not passed.
